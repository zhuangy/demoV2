Here at WebiTap we believe that you should never have to download and install an “app”, because most apps are only useful when you are in certain places or only when you interact with certain objects.
That's why we have developed technologies that allow your phone to 'strem' apps just like videos. You never install them. You get to use them and forget about them. All with a simple tap of your phone.
We do this, with the help of NFC. Near Field Communication allows us to link an app to a tiny microchip called an RFID tag.
Once you tap your NFC enabled phone on the tag, we “stream” the app for you directly to your phone’s web browser.
